# Product Sense

The user is Tree or a future agent trying to turn a demonstrated workflow plus spoken reasoning into a reusable, evidence-bound skill refinement.

## Job

Capture what happened on screen, what Tree said about why it mattered, and how those two streams relate over time, without confusing transcript context for screen evidence.

## Constraints

- Local privacy first.
- Low cognitive load during capture.
- Clear stop/recovery path.
- Evidence artifacts that future agents can actually use.
- No raw-private transcript persistence in durable project files.
- No production/readiness claims without live useful artifacts.

## Acceptance For Product Claims

A positive product claim needs:

- live Record & Replay event artifacts;
- live narrated transcript artifacts;
- generated packet artifacts;
- inspection that the packet is useful for a real skill-refinement task;
- redaction/privacy review;
- claim ceiling recorded in `GOAL_CONTRACT.md` or a completion manifest.

## Human Attention Policy

Escalate to Tree for consent, secrets, microphone access, external publication, raw transcript sharing, or ambiguous private-content handling. Do not escalate routine parsing, fixture failures, missing docs, or ordinary validation decisions.
